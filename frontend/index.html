Importing Account Activities

Follow these steps to import your account activities from a CSV file:

  1. Ensure your CSV file is in the correct format.
  2. Columns should include date, account, symbol, shares, activityType, price, fee
  3. Click the 'Import' button and select your CSV file.
  4. Review the imported activities before confirming.

Supported Activity Types:

  • BUY
  • SELL
  • DEPOSIT
  • WITHDRAWAL
  • DIVIDEND
  • INTEREST
  • FEE

Example CSV format:

date,account,symbol,shares,activityType,price,fee
2024-01-01T15:02:36.329Z,Main Account,MSFT,1,DIVIDEND,57.5,0
2023-12-15T15:02:36.329Z,Trading Account,MSFT,30,BUY,368.60,0
2023-08-11T14:55:30.863Z,Savings Account,$CASH-USD,600.03,DEPOSIT,1,0
2023-06-05T09:15:22.456Z,Savings Account,$CASH-USD,1,INTEREST,180.5,0
2023-05-18T13:45:30.789Z,Trading Account,GOOGL,5,SELL,2500.75,10
2023-04-02T11:20:15.321Z,Main Account,$CASH-USD,1000,WITHDRAWAL,1,0
